If your social media feed suddenly started saying **"Loki forgot to remove her from the timeline,"** you are not alone.

The phrase exploded across X, Instagram, TikTok, and meme pages after **Urvashi Rautela** met **Tom Hiddleston** at Wimbledon 2026.

The internet instantly turned a normal celebrity interaction into a Marvel crossover.

## What does "Loki forgot to remove her from the timeline" mean?

It is a joke based on Marvel's **Loki** series.

In the show, Loki deals with timelines, multiverses, and the Time Variance Authority (TVA), which is responsible for keeping the "Sacred Timeline" intact.

The meme jokes that:

> **Loki successfully manages every timeline... except the one where Urvashi Rautela keeps appearing with international celebrities.**

Obviously, none of this is real.

It is simply internet humor mixing pop culture with a real-life event.

## Where did the meme come from?

The meme started after photos and videos of **Urvashi Rautela** and **Tom Hiddleston** together at Wimbledon began circulating online.

Because Tom Hiddleston is globally known as Loki, Marvel fans immediately connected the moment to the show's timeline jokes.

Within hours, meme pages were posting fake TVA alerts, edited screenshots, and fictional Marvel tweets.

## Why is it funny?

The joke works because it combines two completely unrelated worlds.

One side is a real celebrity appearance.

The other is Marvel's complicated multiverse.

Instead of explaining why the two met, the internet invented a fictional reason:

**Loki forgot to delete this timeline.**

The more dramatic the explanation becomes, the funnier the meme gets.

## Popular versions of the meme

People have created dozens of variations, including:

- "The Sacred Timeline has been compromised."
- "New Nexus Event unlocked."
- "Even the TVA can't stop Urvashi."
- "Loki missed one variant."
- "Tom just got cast in Daaku Maharaj 2."

Most versions pretend the meeting is an official Marvel event.

## Why did it spread so quickly?

The meme had everything internet culture loves.

- A globally recognised Marvel character.
- A real celebrity interaction.
- Simple editing opportunities.
- Endless multiverse jokes.

Anyone familiar with Loki understood the reference immediately, making it easy to remix.

## Do people actually believe it?

No.

Almost everyone sharing the meme understands it is satire.

The joke is not about what actually happened at Wimbledon.

It is about imagining that Marvel's timeline somehow failed because one unexpected celebrity crossover appeared.

## Is this a Marvel meme or a Bollywood meme?

Both.

Marvel fans recognise the TVA, Nexus Events, and the Sacred Timeline.

Indian meme pages recognise Urvashi Rautela's reputation for unexpectedly appearing alongside international celebrities.

Combining those two audiences helped the meme spread beyond Marvel communities.

## Why this meme became so popular

The best internet jokes usually require very little explanation.

"Loki forgot to remove her from the timeline" instantly tells a complete story to anyone who has watched Marvel movies or spent time online.

A celebrity photo became a multiverse event.

The TVA became helpless.

And for a few days, the internet collectively agreed that even Loki cannot control every timeline.
